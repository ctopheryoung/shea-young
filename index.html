How to get here

We are thrilled you're coming to celebrate with us in Portland! If you're flying, the Portland International Airport is great. It is very easy to navigate and only about 20 minutes from Downtown Portland. Once you're on the ground, you have a few options to get to your hotel. The MAX goes directly from the airport to the center of downtown and is only a few dollars to ride. You can also take a cab or airport shuttle to get you into town. We don't suggest renting a car. Most of the hotels are within walking distance of some great restaurants, shops, etc. and all of the venues are easily reachable by Taxi/Uber/Lyft!

Where to stay

We have room blocks at two hotels in the Downtown area that are walking distance to Ecotrust (see below). Please Note: due to the Portland Marathon being held on October 9th, options for accomodations may be limited. We suggest making your travel and hotel reservations sooner rather than later! It can be difficult to find rooms in Portland on busy weekends (like the weekend of our wedding). If neither of the room blocks interest you, it may be worth checking out available places on Airbnb or VRBO. Other hotel options in Downtown include Hotel Modera (starting at $299) or Portland Marriott City Center (also starting at $299) and other options may be available outside of downtown.
